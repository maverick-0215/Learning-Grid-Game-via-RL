The CartPole Problem

A pole is attached by an un-actuated joint to a cart, which moves along a frictionless track. The system is controlled by applying a force of +1 or -1 to the cart. The pendulum starts upright, and the goal is to prevent it from falling over.

State Space (Discretized)

The continuous state space is discretized into bins for tabular RL:

  • Cart Position: 3 bins (Left Edge, Center, Right Edge).
  • Cart Velocity: 3 bins (Moving Left, Still, Moving Right).
  • Pole Angle: 12 bins (Fine-grained from -12° to +12°).
  • Pole Velocity: 6 bins (Fine-grained angular velocity).

Actions

  • Left (0): Push cart to the left.
  • Right (1): Push cart to the right.

Rewards

+1 for every time step that the pole remains upright. The episode ends if the pole falls > 12 degrees or the cart moves > 2.4 units from center.
